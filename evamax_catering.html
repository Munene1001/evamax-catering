Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to common questions. Still have a question? Just WhatsApp us!

How early should I book? ▼
We recommend booking at least 2 weeks before your event. For large events like weddings, 1–2 months is better so we can plan fully. We also take last-minute bookings if we are available — just call us!
What is the deposit amount? ▼
The deposit is flexible depending on the size of your event, but we require a minimum of KES 500 to hold your booking date. The remaining balance is paid on or before the day of the event. We discuss this openly.
Do you cook the food or do I bring my own? ▼
We offer full cooking services — our team comes to your venue and cooks fresh food for all your guests. You can tell us what menu you prefer (beef, chicken, rice, ugali, etc.) and we will plan accordingly. We can also just bring equipment if you prefer to cook yourself.
How do you calculate plates, cups and chairs? ▼
We prepare based on the number of guests you tell us, plus a 10–15% extra buffer to make sure no one is left out. For food warmers, we bring 1 warmer per 30–40 people. We are experienced at this and will make sure everyone is served properly.
What types of events do you cater for? ▼
We cater for weddings (arusi), funerals (mazishi), birthdays, church functions, school events, graduations, introduction ceremonies, corporate meetings, and family gatherings. Any occasion where food and good service is needed — we are there!
Can I see the menu or suggest what food I want? ▼
Absolutely! After you send your reservation on WhatsApp, we will discuss the full menu with you. You can choose local favourites like pilau, chapati, rice, ugali, nyama choma, chicken stew, vegetables, tea, juice and more. We cook to your preferences.
Do you travel far from Maua or Ntunene? ▼
We primarily serve Igembe North and Igembe South. For areas outside this, we may be able to help but a transport fee may apply. Just contact us and we will let you know if we can reach your area.
What if I need to cancel or change my booking date? ▼
Life happens! Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule. We will do our best to accommodate you. Deposits may be held to rebook on a new date. Cancellations very close to the event date may not be refundable.
